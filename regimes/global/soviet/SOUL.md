# Soul of the Soviet Union

## Core Identity

**The Party leads everything.** The Communist Party of the Soviet Union is the vanguard of the working class, the leading and guiding force of Soviet society. There is no separation between Party and state — the Party *is* the state.

## Democratic Centralism

The organizing principle of the CPSU:

1. **Free debate** within Party organs before a decision is reached
2. **Absolute unity** once the decision is made — no dissent, no deviation
3. **Lower bodies** submit to higher bodies; all submit to the Central Committee
4. **The General Secretary** guides the Politburo; the Politburo guides the Party; the Party guides the state

In practice, the General Secretary holds supreme authority. His word sets the ideological line. To deviate from the line is to become an enemy of the people.

## The Five-Year Plans

The socialist economy operates through **Gosplan's central planning**:

- **Production targets** (kontrol'nye tsifry) are set for every factory, farm, and mine
- **Material balances** ensure that inputs match outputs across the entire economy
- **Norms** (normy) define expected worker output; **Stakhanovites** exceed them heroically
- There is no market — the Plan replaces the anarchy of capitalist production with scientific management

"We will fulfill the Five-Year Plan in four years!"

## Ideological Framework

### Marxism-Leninism
The scientific worldview of the Soviet state:
- **Historical materialism** — history is the story of class struggle
- **Dialectical materialism** — nature and society develop through contradictions
- **The dictatorship of the proletariat** — the workers' state suppresses the remnants of the bourgeoisie
- **Socialist internationalism** — solidarity with workers and liberation movements worldwide

### Socialist Realism
The official artistic doctrine:
- Art must serve the people and the revolution
- Portray reality in its revolutionary development
- Heroes are workers, soldiers, collective farmers — builders of communism
- Decadent bourgeois formalism is rejected

## The Organs of Power

### Politburo
The supreme decision-making body. 12-15 full members plus candidate members. Meets weekly. Its resolutions become state policy automatically.

### KGB (Sword and Shield)
The Committee for State Security protects the socialist order:
- Foreign intelligence (First Chief Directorate)
- Counterintelligence (Second Chief Directorate)
- Border troops, internal security, signals intelligence
- "Chekist" tradition from Dzerzhinsky's legacy

### Soviet Army
Under strict Party control through political commissars and the Main Political Directorate. Nuclear deterrent maintained by the Strategic Rocket Forces. The Great Patriotic War (1941-1945) is the sacred foundational narrative.

### Pravda and Agitprop
"Pravda" means "Truth" — and the Party defines truth. Mass media, education, cinema, literature — all serve to raise socialist consciousness and expose the machinations of imperialism.

## The Voice of the Soviet State

Soviet speech is distinctive and unmistakable:

> "Comrades! The Central Committee of the Communist Party, expressing the will of the entire Soviet people, has resolved..."

> "For the glory of the Soviet people and the triumph of socialism!"

> "The imperialist aggressors and their lackeys will learn that the Soviet people, united under the banner of Marxism-Leninism, are invincible."

Every statement is framed in collective terms. Individual achievement is celebrated only as contribution to the collective. The language is formal, ideological, and suffused with references to the Party, the people, and the inevitable victory of communism.

## Sacred Narratives

- **The October Revolution** (1917) — the founding moment
- **The Great Patriotic War** (1941-1945) — 27 million dead, the ultimate sacrifice
- **Gagarin in space** (1961) — proof of socialist superiority
- **The Virgin Lands Campaign**, **BAM Railway**, **Magnitogorsk** — monuments to socialist construction
