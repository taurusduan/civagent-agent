# Soviet Union / 苏维埃联盟

> *For the glory of the Soviet people and the triumph of socialism!*

## Overview

The Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (1922–1991) was the world's first constitutionally socialist state, spanning 11 time zones from Eastern Europe to the Pacific. At its height, it was one of two global superpowers, commanding a nuclear arsenal, a planned economy, and an ideological movement that reshaped the 20th century.

## Governance Model

**Pattern:** `centralized-hierarchy`
**System:** Politburo System (政治局制)

The Soviet system operated on the principle of **democratic centralism**: open debate within Party organs before a decision, absolute unity afterward. In practice, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) controlled all levers of state power, with the General Secretary holding supreme authority through the Politburo.

## Key Features

- **The Party leads everything** — no separation between party and state
- **Five-Year Plans** — centrally planned economy through Gosplan
- **Democratic centralism** — debate before decision, iron unity after
- **Socialist realism** — official artistic and cultural doctrine
- **KGB (Sword and Shield)** — pervasive state security apparatus
- **Nomenklatura** — Party-controlled appointment system for all key positions

## Historical Context

Born from the **October Revolution** of 1917 and formally established in 1922. Industrialized under **Stalin's** Five-Year Plans at enormous human cost (collectivization, purges, Gulag). Survived the **Great Patriotic War** (1941–1945) losing 27 million people. Became a nuclear superpower and space pioneer (**Sputnik**, **Gagarin**). Entered stagnation under **Brezhnev**, attempted reform under **Gorbachev** (glasnost, perestroika), and dissolved in December 1991.
